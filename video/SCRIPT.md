## Pronunciation guide for TTS

- DEF 14A: **"def fourteen A"**
- CEO: **"C E O"**
- ISS: **"I S S"** (spell out)
- Glass Lewis: **"glass loo-iss"**
- Broadridge: **"broad-ridge"**
- speakup.vote: **"speak up dot vote"**
- iwbinb: **"i-w-bin-b"** (literal spelling)
- Numbers spoken in full ("seventy percent", "fifty million", "fifty-six billion")
- Hyphenated compound spelled out: "twenty twenty-five" not "two thousand twenty-five"

## Tone notes

- First line is the hook — must land in 3 seconds. Don't fade in.
- Half-beat pause between each `>` paragraph. Don't rush.
- Beat 5 (Musk) is the emotional peak. Slow down 5%, lean into the numbers.
- Closing line ("Give every on-chain shareholder a voice") said warmly, not loud.
