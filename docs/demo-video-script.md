## Tone notes

- **Pace**: conversational, not auctioneer. 145 wpm.
- **Energy**: confident, not hyped. Don't oversell.
- **Voice**: speak like you are showing a smart friend, not pitching VCs.
- **Pauses**: half a beat after every sentence ends. Resist the urge to fill silence.
- **Pronunciation**:
  - DEF 14A: "def fourteen A"
  - ISS: spell it out, eye-ess-ess
  - Glass Lewis: rhymes with "Lewis"
  - Broadridge: "broad-ridge"
  - speakup-vote: when ready, say "speak up dot vote"
